Navigation -  Selects the visualization

1) Chronological Categorical lines

Interactions:

Events leading to Sahel's current condition can roughly be divided into 4 rubrics.

  • The left side lists the 4 general topics
  • Composed entirely of divs, text, and images
  • Each pip starts as a transparent circle with a categorical border color
  • On hover: pip grows larger, reveals a picture, and event title displays below
  • On click: Changes: event information, data information, and visualization

2) Chronological network graph

In the event that I can identify convincing supporting information that single events allowed or significantly influenced certain future outcomes I would use a network graph

  • Composed of SVG elements using D3
  • Each pip remains expanded with an image or icon within
  • On hover: shows a popup of title and category of event
  • On click: Changes: event information, data information, and visualization

Visualization

1) Country with embedded heatmap

When showing a small number (>5) of indicators for each country

When foregrounding regionality of phenomena

  • Each country has a constantly on popup
  • Each popup contains one or more heatmap squares
  • Each heatmap color corresponds to the navigator categorical color
  • Squares can be bisected so the top indicates the value and the bottom indicates the % change year over year
  • Hovering over an indicator changes the border paths of the countries to their respective colors for the given indicator
  • The year for the indicators is printed at the top
  • Arrows allow you to manually increment/decrement the year
  • Hovering on the year reveals a play button which plays the sequence of years automatically

2) Country with external heatmap

When showing a medium number (>20) of indicators for each country

When foregrounding regionality of phenomena

Same as above except

  • Avoids clutter of large popups
  • Separates the heatmap and map
  • Each remain responsive to the others events

3) Bars with all years at a glance

When regionality is less important and progression year over year should be seen at a glance.

For showing > 10 indicators

None of the bars have Y axes so it is useful to show a magnitude delta.

  • The legend associates colors with indicator(s)
  • Colors are used for each indicator bar
  • Hovering over any bar will highlight the same indicator across all years/countries

Events (Terrorism or Duration of UN Initiatives)

1) Colored line events moving left with time.  

Lines scrolling from right to left below a visualization to show the introduction of and ongoing status of UN initiatives.  Correlates funding with change in regional phenomena.

  • The leading edge of each bar has initiative title and funding change YoY.
  • Bar color corresponds to the established categorical colors
  • Color varies with % funding change YoY
  • Hover over title shows popup of actual funding information

2) Leaderboard of events on right side of vis

Shows UN initiatives in a double columned leaderboard to the right of the visualization.  More appropriate to show differences in absolute magnitude of funding.

  • Leaderboard changes with year
  • Sorts initiatives by absolute funding $
